About The School Of Psychology

The School of Psychology at Consolata International University (CIU) blends scientific excellence with faith-inspired compassion to shape ethical professionals ready to serve society. Guided by the Consolata Missionary tradition, the School nurtures learners who are intellectually grounded, spiritually formed, and socially responsive.

We believe psychology is more than a discipline — it is a calling to heal, guide, and empower others.

Our programs equip students to apply psychological science with empathy and moral integrity in schools, hospitals, churches, and communities across Kenya and beyond.

“To study Psychology at CIU is to discover the science of the mind, the beauty of empathy, and the mission of service.”

Programs Offered:

🎓 Bachelor of Arts in Counselling Psychology (B.A.)
Duration: 4 Years Mode:: Face to face, Blended /Online
A flagship program that combines academic rigor, faith integration, and supervised practice. Students are formed to be compassionate counselors, effective communicators, and ethical leaders.
Accreditation: Commission for University Education (CUE), Kenya
Regulatory Alignment: Counsellors & Psychologists Act (2014)

🎓 Diploma in Counselling Psychology
Duration: 2 Years Mode: Face to face, Blended /Online
Prepares learners for entry-level professional counseling and community support roles, emphasizing applied skills, supervision, and ethics.

🎓 Certificate in Counselling Psychology
Duration: 1 Year Mode:: Face to face, Blended /Online
An ideal foundation for those exploring the field of psychology, parish counseling, or youth mentorship. It provides the basic competencies for personal and community service.
