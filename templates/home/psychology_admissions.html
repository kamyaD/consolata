Why Study at CIU?

  • Faith + Science Integration – Explore the human mind through both evidence and spirituality.
  • Hands-On Learning – Train in modern counseling labs and complete supervised internships in schools, hospitals, and NGOs.
  • Personal Mentorship – Grow under the guidance of licensed professionals and clergy mentors.
  • Mission-Driven Formation – Transform your learning into community service and outreach.
  • Professional Recognition – CUE-accredited, nationally recognized, and ethically compliant.

Graduate Outcome

CIU Psychology graduates are prepared to work as:

  • School & Guidance Counselors
  • Clinical & Community Counselors
  • HR & Wellness Officers
  • Faith-Based Program Officers
  • Behavioral Researchers & Educators

They leave empowered by wisdom, grounded in faith, and dedicated to service.

Admissions:

Intakes: January. May. September

Entry Requirements:

  • KCSE C+ (plus) or equivalent for Bachelor’s program
  • KCSE C- ( minus) for Diploma program
  • KCSE D (plain ) for Certificate program
  • Diploma holders may qualify for credit transfers
