About the School of Theology

At CIU School of Theology, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional theological formation that nurtures the mind, strengthens the spirit, and prepares mission agents, well equipped to carry out the mission of the church in the world, with compassion, wisdom, and integrity.

Rooted in a deep commitment to Catholic faith, theological and missionary formation, our programs blend rigorous academic training with meaningful preparation for mission and ministry in the ever-changing world and society today.

We offer a dynamic environment with a rich international/intercultural richness that allows our students to widen their horizon and grow in the spirit of living and interacting with people from different countries, cultures, languages, and social situations.

Our faculty, composed of experienced Priests and Religious, is passionate about mentoring students to become leaders who can think critically, serve selflessly, and contribute positively to the global mission of the church in the society.

Whether you are preparing for pastoral ministry, academic research, or community leadership, our CIU School of Theology provides a supportive, transformative space where your faith and intellectual growth and missionary spirit can flourish together, empowering you to make a lasting impact in your ministry in the world today.

Motto

“EMPOWERED BY WISDOM, GROUNDED IN FAITH, DEDICATED TO SERVICE”

Mission

To provide transformative educational experience that prepares leaders who are not only academically proficient to thrive in a rapidly changing world, but also compassionate, ethical and socially responsible. Rooted in the rich tradition of the Consolata Missionaries, we strive to foster a dynamic learning community where intellectual curiosity is encouraged, spiritual growth is nurtured, and a passion for the mission and service to others is ignited. We are committed to creating a brighter future for all, where knowledge is a force for good, faith is a source of resilience, and service is a way of life.

Spiritual Formation & Guidance

The School of Theology offers opportunities for regular spiritual services, prayer groups, retreats, and personal spiritual direction. These experiences nurture the spiritual lives of students, helping them grow in faith and deepen their relationship with God.

Mentorship & Vocation Guidance

Our Faculty members, experienced Priests and Religious, often serve as mentors to students. The students are helped to reflect deeply on their personal faith journey and vocation.
